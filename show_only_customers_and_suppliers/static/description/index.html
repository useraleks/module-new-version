You can filter or show only customers or suppliers in purchases and sales

This module is parameterized which means that you can not only filter by clients and suppliers, you can filter by any other label or tag.

In Conf > Sales You can activate filtering for sales.
In Conf > Purchase You also can activate filtering for Purchase orders.
As you can se in contacts we have only one contact with the Tag "Client".
On Sales > Customers, Only clients appear, this can also be done in suppliers.
And finally, On the Customer Field will only display the contacts with the Clients Tag
